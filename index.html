About Me

I'm a PhD Student in the Programming Languages Group at the Johannes Gutenberg-University Mainz under the supervision of Sebastian Erdweg. I received my Bachelor's and Master's degree in computer science at TU Darmstadt in 2015 and 2018, respectively. I currently focus my research on deriving incremental type checkers based on traditional ones. Other interests include domain-specific languages, type systems, and functional programming.
